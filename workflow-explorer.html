Technical Workflow System

A structured pipeline for turning ideas into verified production code. Three work types — Feature, Bugfix, and Epic — each follow their own pipeline through phases of exploration, decisions, validated specifications, implementation planning, test-driven development, and final review.

Run /workflow-start to see all active work, or /start-feature, /start-bugfix, /start-epic to begin.
